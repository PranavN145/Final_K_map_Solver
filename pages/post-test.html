  • A) They represent invalid Boolean expressions
  • B) They must always be grouped as 1s
  • C) They must always be grouped as 0s
  • D) They can be treated as either 0 or 1 to form larger groups for better minimization

  • A) A'B
  • B) A'B'
  • C) AB'
  • D) CD'

  • B) Cells containing don't cares only
  • C) Cells containing 1s (and optionally don't cares)
  • D) All cells regardless of their value

  • C) A'B'
  • D) 1 (always true)
